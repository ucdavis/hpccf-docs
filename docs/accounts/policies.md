## Separation from the University

Use and access to HPC@UCD resources are contingent on active affiliation with the university as staff, student, faculty,
or possessing an active [TAF](https://taf.ucdavis.edu/). We will notify users when their affiliation with UC Davis has
lapsed, warning them that their account will be deactivated in 1 month unless their affiliation is renewed (via
[TAF](https://taf.ucdavis.edu/) or another mechanism). After 6 months, deactivated account home directories will be
archived and accessible only by [contacting HPC@UCD](../support.md).
